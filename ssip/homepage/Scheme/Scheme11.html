Details of Loan for Study abroad


Many Indian students share the common goal to study abroad. While overseas education can be expensive, Indian students can finance their higher education through loans offered by Indian banks and financial institutions specifically for study abroad programs.


Study abroad education loans are available for Indian students pursuing graduate and post-graduate programs, and technical courses in recognized universities and professional institutions overseas. These courses can include: Bachelor’s degree programs Master's degree programs Medicine Law Business and management programs such as MBAs Accounting courses such as CIMA-London or CPA-USA. Skill development training in performing arts, fashion, and design. Other Job-oriented professional or technical courses.

  1. Financial Support: The primary objective of the Gujarat State Education Loan Scheme is to provide financial assistance to students who wish to pursue higher education in recognized institutions abroad
  2. Access to Education: The scheme aims to promote equal access to education by providing loans to students from economically weaker sections of society.
  3. Skill Development: The scheme focuses on promoting skill development among students by supporting their pursuit of professional and technical courses. It encourages students to acquire the necessary skills and knowledge required for employment in various sectors, thereby enhancing their employability and contributing to the overall development of the state.
  4. Encouraging Meritorious Students: The Gujarat State Education Loan Scheme aims to recognize and support meritorious students who have excelled academically. It provides education loans at such as lower interest rates for students who have secured admissions to reputed institutions or have achieved exceptional academic performance.
  5. Maximum Loan Amount Guaranteed Up to INR 15,00,000 at 4% interest rate

  1. The student must be an Indian national.
  2. Graduate degree courses require the students to complete Standard XII.
  3. Students completing Standard X can apply for diploma courses abroad.
  4. Students pursuing a postgraduate degree abroad must have a qualifying degree from an Indian university (10 plus 2 plus 3 or 10 plus 2 plus 4).
  5. The student must have been accepted into the university, college, or program abroad.

Application Process

This is the application process content.
Step 1: To initiate the application process, the student must first create an account on the portal by providing basic information, such as name, date of birth, email address, and mobile number.
Step 2: Once the account is created, the student can access the application form, which requires detailed information about themselves, their family, education, and the course for which they are seeking a loan.
Step 3: Upon completing the application form and uploading all the required documents, the student can submit the application online. The portal also enables students to track the status of their application, receive notifications and communicate with relevant authorities.
Step 4: The authorities will review the application and notify the student of their decision. If approved, the student will be required to sign an agreement and provide additional documents, such as bank account and collateral details (if applicable).

Details of Saraswati Sadhana Yojana


• Providing transportation facilities to the girl students of class 9th studying in government schools and aided non-government schools
• Encouraging girl child education.
• To reduce the school dropout rate under this scheme, the state government has decided to give cycles to girls.
• There is also an annual income limit for beneficiaries under Saraswati Sadhana Scheme which is :-
Rs. 1,20,000/- in Rural Area Residents.
Rs. 1,50,000/- in Urban Area Residents.
• Girls will be able to go to school if the school is away from them by getting cycles.

  1. Free cycles are being distributed to girl students under scheme.

• Permanent Residents of Gujarat.
• Applicant should be Girl Student.
• Girl Student should be studying in Class 9th.
• The Annual Family Income of Girl Student should be :-
• Rs. 1,20,000/- in Rural Area.
• Rs. 1,50,000/- in Urban Area.
• Girl Student should be belong to Schedule Caste or Developing Caste.

1. Eligible Girl Beneficiary doesn't have to apply anywhere to avail the benefit of free of cost bicyce under Gujarat Sarawati Sadhana Yojana.
2. Principal of School in which girl is studying will prepare the list of eligible girl beneficiaries.
3. Principal of School will then upload the list of eligible girl beneficiaries in Digital Gujarat Portal of Gujarat Government.
4. Recommended Applications of Eligible Beneficiary will then verified by District Social Welfare Officer.
5. After verification, Department will generate and disburse vouchers for beneficiaries to get free of cost bicycle.
6. Beneficiary has to visit the authorised bicycle dealer with the received voucher to get the free of cost bicycle.

  1. Domicile/ Residence Proof of Gujarat.
  2. Aadhar Card of Girl.
  3. Aadhar Card of Parents.
  4. Caste Certificate.
  5. Income Certificate.
  6. Bank Account Details.
  7. 8th Class Mark Sheet
